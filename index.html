About Us :

JSPM Engineering College was established in 2011 and has since then been at the forefront of engineering education in the region. We offer undergraduate and graduate degree programs in a variety of engineering disciplines.

  • Ranked among the top 10 engineering colleges in the country
  • State-of-the-art facilities and equipment
  • Experienced and dedicated faculty members
  • Strong industry partnerships for internships and job placements

Our mission is to provide our students with a comprehensive education in engineering that prepares them for successful careers and lifelong learning.

    • Placement :

  • Engineering colleges often have career services offices that help students prepare for and find employment after graduation.
  • Placement opportunities are available in various industries, including manufacturing, construction, telecommunications, energy, and aerospace.
  • Engineering colleges often have partnerships with industry partners, providing students with internships, co-op experiences, and other work-based learning opportunities.
  • Engineering college graduates are often in high demand due to their technical skills, problem-solving abilities, and ability to work in teams.
  • Graduates of engineering colleges often hold positions such as design engineer, process engineer, research engineer, project engineer, or systems engineer.
  • Salaries for engineering college graduates are often higher than the average for other professions.
  • Overall, engineering colleges play a critical role in advancing engineering knowledge, preparing students for successful careers in engineering, and contributing to the economic development of local and global communities.
